Welcome to My Exercise Plan!


Are you ready to get started on your fitness journey? This simple exercise plan will help you build a foundation for a healthier and more active lifestyle.


Day 1: Walking


Start with a 15-minute brisk walk around your neighborhood or a nearby park. Focus on maintaining good posture and breathing rhythmically.


Day 2: Stretching


Perform a 10-minute stretching routine to improve flexibility. Include stretches for your neck, shoulders, arms, legs, and back.


Day 3: Squats


Do 3 sets of 10 squats. Squats are great for building lower body strength. Keep your back straight, and go as low as you comfortably can.


Day 4: Rest


Rest is essential for muscle recovery. Take a break today and focus on staying hydrated.
